Controls

Movement keys: W A S D or arrow keys
Standard attack: Left clicking or pressing the 1 key will perform a standard attack in direction the player is facing
Whirlwind attack: Right clicking or pressing the 2 key will perform a sweeping attack around the player.
Consume healing potion: Pressing the H key will consume a potion and restore your health.
Respawn: Pressing the space key will respawn your character at the beginning of the level
Mute audio: Pressing the M key will mute and unmute audio.
Toggle debug mode: Pressing the ~ (tilde) key will toggle debug mode on and off.

Information

Compatible with Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.
Internet Explorer is incompatible due to lack of ES6 support.
